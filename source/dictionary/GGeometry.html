Geometry

The top-level geometric properties of the simulation. Properties specified here will carry over to all nanoverse.runtime.layers of the simulation.

MemberTypeDescription
shapeShapeThe boundary shape and size associated with all nanoverse.runtime.layers of this simulation. This does not necessarily need to match the lattice topology, although they must be compatible. For example, one could use a TriangularLattice with a Rectangle shape, but not with a Cuboid.
latticeLatticeThe lattice topology (square, triangular, etc) to be used for all nanoverse.runtime.layers in this simulation.
