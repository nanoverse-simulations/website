Crosshairs

A crosshairs glyph appears as a circle overlaid with a symmetrical cross.

MemberTypeDescription
colorStringHexadecimal representation of the glyph's color (RRGGBB).
crossDoubleSize of the cross of the crosshairs, specified as a multiple of the size of the circle component of the crosshairs.
circleDoubleSize of the circle of the crosshairs, specified as a multiple of the size of the lattice site in the visualization.
