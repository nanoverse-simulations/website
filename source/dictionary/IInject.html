Inject

Schedule a fixed-value source (injection) at a site or sites of a specified continuum layer.

MemberTypeDescription
periodIntegerWaiting period between occurrences of this process. period=0 means do only once.
startIntegerTime at which to begin executing this process (specified in integration cycles).
valueDoubleValue to inject at each affected site.
layerStringContinuum layer upon which to schedule injection process.
activeSitesCoordinateSetSites that this process should update.
