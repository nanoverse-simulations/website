CorrelationWriter

Calculate the pairwise correlation between individuals at every distance. This calculation is used to measure spatial structure. The results are measured at a specified time point, and aggregated over all simulations.

MemberTypeDescription
filenameStringThe filename for the report.
timeIntegerThe time (in number of integration cycles) at which the correlation should be calculated. Specify one CorrelationWriter for each time point at which a measurement is desired.
