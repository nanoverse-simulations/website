shape

The boundary shape and size associated with all nanoverse.runtime.layers of this simulation. This does not necessarily need to match the lattice topology, although they must be compatible. For example, one could use a TriangularLattice with a Rectangle shape, but not with a Cuboid.

MemberTypeDescription
LineLineA line is a 1D boundary with two endpoints.
HexagonHexagonA hexagon is a 2D boundary with three sets of parallel sides, all equidistant from a center point. Requires a Triangular lattice.
RectangleRectangleA rectangle is a 2D boundary with two sets of parallel sides.
CuboidCuboidA cuboid is a 3D boundary with three sets of parallel sides.
